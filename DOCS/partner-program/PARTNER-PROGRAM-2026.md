## 1. Executive Summary

ConformEdge is an AI-powered ISO Compliance Management platform built for South African construction and infrastructure companies. Our Partner Program enables SHEQ consultancies, compliance firms, and industry specialists to manage multiple client organisations from a single platform — at significantly lower cost than individual subscriptions.

**Three partner tiers** serve different business models:

| Tier | Best For | Starting Cost |
|------|----------|---------------|
| Referral | Independent advisors who recommend, not manage | Free |
| Consulting | Firms managing compliance for multiple clients | R4,999/mo + R899/client |
| White-Label | Large consultancies wanting their own brand | R6,999/mo + R699/client |

## 2. The Opportunity

### The Problem

SHEQ consultancies in South Africa typically manage ISO compliance for 10–30 client companies. Today, each client would need their own ConformEdge subscription:

- **Essentials:** R2,299/mo per client
- **Professional:** R4,499/mo per client
- **Business:** R8,499/mo per client

A firm managing 15 clients on Professional tier: **15 x R4,499 = R67,485/mo**

This creates two problems:
1. **Clients won't buy it themselves** — they're paying the consultant to handle compliance
2. **Per-client pricing is expensive** — consultants need margin to run their business

### The Solution

The Partner Program provides:
- **Multi-tenant dashboard** — manage all clients from one login
- **Volume pricing** — significantly below retail per-client cost
- **Isolated tenants** — each client's data is completely separate
- **Professional tools** — bulk scheduling, consolidated reporting, health scores

## 4. Tier 1 — Referral Partner

### Overview
For independent consultants, industry associations, and professionals who recommend compliance solutions but don't manage day-to-day operations.

### How It Works
1. Sign up for a free referral partner account
2. Receive a unique referral tracking link
3. Share the link with prospective clients
4. Client signs up directly for any ConformEdge plan
5. You earn 15% commission on the client's first year of subscription

### Commission Structure

| Client Plan | Monthly | Your Annual Commission (15%) |
|-------------|---------|------------------------------|
| Essentials (R2,299/mo) | R2,299 | R4,138 per client |
| Professional (R4,499/mo) | R4,499 | R8,098 per client |
| Business (R8,499/mo) | R8,499 | R15,298 per client |

### Example
Refer 5 clients who sign up for Professional:
- 5 x R4,499 x 12 months x 15% = **R40,491/year commission**
- No ongoing management required

### Requirements
- Valid business registration or professional membership
- Basic familiarity with ISO compliance standards
- No minimum referral count

For SHEQ consultancies, compliance management firms, and quality professionals who actively manage ISO compliance for multiple client companies.

## 11. Onboarding Process

### Referral Partner (1 day)
1. Complete online application form
2. Receive referral tracking link
3. Start referring

### Consulting Partner (1–2 weeks)
1. **Application:** Submit partner inquiry form with business details
2. **Discovery call:** 30-minute call to understand your client base and needs
3. **Agreement:** Sign partner agreement and SLA
4. **Setup:** Platform account created, internal users provisioned
5. **Training:** 2-hour online certification session (free)
6. **Client migration:** Assisted setup of first 3 client organisations
7. **Go live:** Full access to partner dashboard and tools

### White-Label Partner (2–4 weeks)
1. Steps 1–4 as above
2. **Branding workshop:** Provide logo, colours, domain for white-label setup

4. **Advanced training:** 4-hour session covering API, white-label admin, reporting
5. **Pilot:** 2-week pilot with 2–3 client orgs

## 13. Revenue Model for Partners

### How Consultants Profit

The partner program is designed to be a profit centre, not just a cost saving.

**Typical consulting business model:**

| Line Item | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Client charges for "compliance management service" | R4,000–R7,000/mo per client |
| Your cost on ConformEdge per client | R899/mo (Consulting) or R699/mo (White-Label) |
| **Your margin per client** | **R3,101–R6,301/mo** |

### Example: 15-Client Consulting Firm

| Revenue Stream | Monthly | Annual |
|----------------|---------|--------|
| Client fees (15 x R5,000 avg) | R75,000 | R900,000 |
| ConformEdge cost (platform + 15 orgs) | R17,134 | R205,608 |
| **Net margin from platform** | **R57,866** | **R694,392** |

The platform pays for itself many times over while reducing your manual workload through AI-powered document classification, automated gap analysis, and bulk scheduling.

### Network Effect Revenue

Your clients' subcontractors interact with the platform through the Subcontractor Portal. Some will convert to their own ConformEdge subscriptions:

1. You onboard 15 clients
2. Each client has ~10 subcontractors using the portal
3. ~5% convert to Essentials plans (R2,299/mo)
4. That's 7–8 new customers you helped create — strengthening the ecosystem

## 15. Terms & Conditions Summary

### Contract Terms
- **Referral:** No contract, cancel anytime
- **Consulting:** 12-month minimum, then month-to-month
- **White-Label:** 24-month minimum, then annual renewal

### Payment
- Monthly invoicing via bank transfer or debit order
- Annual prepayment: 17% discount (pay 10 months for 12)
- 30-day payment terms for established partners

### Client Data
- Each client org's data is isolated and belongs to the client
- Partners access client data only with client consent
- If a client leaves the partner, data can be migrated to a direct subscription
- Data export available in standard formats (CSV, PDF)

### Intellectual Property
- ConformEdge platform remains ISU Technologies property
- White-label branding is licensed, not transferred
- Partner-created templates and content remain partner property

### Termination
- Partners may terminate with 60-day notice
- Client organisations can transition to direct subscriptions
- Data portability guaranteed for 90 days post-termination
