## 1. Executive Summary

ConformEdge is an AI-powered ISO Compliance Management platform built for South African construction and infrastructure companies. Our Partner Program enables SHEQ consultancies, compliance firms, and safety practitioners to manage multiple client organisations from a single platform.

**Simple pricing. No hidden fees. No volume discount complexity.**

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Setup & training (once-off) | R25,000 |
| Per consultant seat | R999/mo |
| Per client organisation | R1,499 – R2,999/mo (depends on features) |
| Annual billing discount | 2 months free (pay 10, get 12) |

You start with 5 consultant seats and add client organisations as you grow. Your cost to ConformEdge scales predictably — no surprises.

## 2. The Opportunity

### The Problem

SHEQ consultancies in South Africa typically manage ISO compliance for 5–30 client companies. Today, each client would need their own ConformEdge subscription:

- **Essentials:** R2,299/mo per client
- **Professional:** R5,499/mo per client
- **Business:** R8,499/mo per client

A firm managing 15 clients on Professional tier: **15 × R5,499 = R82,485/mo**

This creates two problems:
1. **Clients won't buy it themselves** — they're paying the consultant to handle compliance
2. **Per-client pricing is expensive** — consultants need margin to run their business

### The Solution

The Partner Program gives you:
- **Multi-tenant dashboard** — manage all clients from one login
- **Flat per-client pricing** — significantly below retail, with no volume tiers to track
- **Isolated tenants** — each client's data is completely separate
- **Professional tools** — incident management, work permits, equipment register, AI-powered gap analysis
- **Your own business model** — charge your clients whatever the market bears, keep the margin

## 3. How It Works

### Step 1: Setup

You pay a once-off **R25,000 setup fee**. We configure your partner account, provision your consultant seats, set up your first 5 client organisations, and deliver 12 hours of structured training.

### Step 2: Operate

Your consultants log in, switch between client organisations, and do the work — logging incidents, issuing work permits, running safety checklists, managing equipment registers, generating compliance reports.

### Step 3: Grow

Add more client organisations at the flat per-org rate. Need more consultants? Add seats at R999/mo each. Your cost scales linearly and predictably.

### Two Ways Your Team Works

**Partner Console** — bird's-eye view across ALL your clients. Compliance health scores, overdue items, risk alerts, billing overview. Use this for oversight and reporting.

**Client Workspace** — switch into any client's organisation to do hands-on work. Create incidents, issue permits, complete checklists, manage documents. This is where the daily safety work happens.

## 8. Your Revenue Model

### How You Profit

The Partner Program is designed to be a **profit centre**, not just a cost saving. You charge your construction clients for compliance management services — the platform is your operational tool.

| Line Item | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| You charge your client (compliance management service) | R4,000–R8,000/mo per client |
| Your ConformEdge cost per client (Professional) | R1,999/mo |
| **Your margin per client** | **R2,001–R6,001/mo** |

### Example: 10-Client Practice (Annual Billing)

| | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|
| Client fees (10 × R5,000) | R50,000 | R600,000 |
| ConformEdge cost (annual billing) | R20,821 | R249,850 |
| **Your gross profit** | **R29,179** | **R350,150** |

After deducting 2 consultant salaries (R50,000/mo), you still net **R100,150/year** — and that's at only R5K/client. Charge R6K–R7K and the numbers get significantly better.

### What You Charge Is Your Business

We don't dictate or cap what you charge your clients. Most SHEQ consultancies in SA charge R4,000–R8,000/mo for compliance management services. Your ConformEdge cost (R1,999/client on Professional) leaves healthy margin at any price point.

### Network Effect

Your clients' subcontractors interact with the platform through the Subcontractor Portal. Some convert to their own ConformEdge subscriptions — strengthening the ecosystem and your value proposition.
