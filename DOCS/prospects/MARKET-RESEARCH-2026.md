### Government Procurement — How to Sell

**Prerequisites:**
1. CSD Registration (secure.csd.gov.za) — FREE, mandatory
2. B-BBEE Certificate/Affidavit — EME needs sworn affidavit only
3. SARS Tax Compliance Pin — must be valid
4. SBD Forms — Standard tender documentation

**Three Channels:**
1. **Municipal SCM** (easiest) — Municipalities procure independently, no SITA required
2. **Direct SOE Procurement** — SOEs have independent PFMA procurement
3. **SITA Transversal Contract** (highest leverage) — 12-18 month process, gives access to ALL national/provincial departments

### Priority Sales Sequence

3. **Cape Town or eThekwini metro** — Better-governed, simpler procurement
4. **Eskom single division** — Proof of concept before group-wide pitch
5. **SITA transversal contract** — Long-term play (12-18 months)
