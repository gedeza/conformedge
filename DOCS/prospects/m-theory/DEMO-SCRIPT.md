## PART 17: Q&A & Discussion (10-15 minutes)

### Likely Questions

**"Which tier suits us best?"**
> "Based on what you've told me about your practice — Consulting Partner. You get the multi-tenant dashboard, per-client isolation, and volume pricing. If you later want your own brand, we upgrade to White-Label. No data migration needed."

**"How do our clients access the platform?"**
> "Each client gets their own isolated environment with 3 user accounts included. They see only their data. You see all of them. They don't need to know about ConformEdge or the partner relationship unless you want them to."

**"What about user licensing — can I add more users?"**
> "Yes. Your team gets 5 consultant users included. Extra consultants are R149/mo each. Each client org gets 3 users included. Extra client users are R99/mo each. Five roles are available — Owner, Admin, Manager, Auditor, Viewer — so you control access granularly."

**"How does AI classification work?"**
> "Anthropic's Claude AI reads the document content, identifies the ISO standard, maps it to the correct clause, and returns a confidence score. Documents are encrypted in transit and stored on Cloudflare R2. Each client org gets 100 AI credits per month supplementary to the plan."

**"What if a client leaves our practice?"**
> "Their data stays isolated. We can transition them to a direct ConformEdge subscription — no data loss, no re-upload. You disconnect them from your Partner Console and they continue independently."

**"Can we try before committing?"**
> "Absolutely. I'll set up a trial Consulting Partner account. We load 2-3 of your existing clients, upload some of their documents, and let the AI classify them. You'll see the value in the first hour."

**"What about the frameworks we discussed — ECSA, SACPCMP?"**
> "They're on our roadmap. If M-Theory's clients need them, we can prioritise development. As a Consulting Partner, you get advisory input on our product roadmap."

**"How does the referral commission work alongside consulting?"**
> "They're complementary. If a prospect approaches you but doesn't need your consulting services — maybe they want to self-manage — you share your referral link. They sign up directly, and you earn 15% of their subscription for 12 months. A Professional referral is worth over R8,000/year to you."

**"What about data security?"**
> "Clerk authentication with multi-factor, role-based access control, encrypted file storage on Cloudflare R2, full audit trail. Each client's data is completely isolated — a breach in one tenant cannot affect another. We're building toward ISO 27001 for the platform itself."
