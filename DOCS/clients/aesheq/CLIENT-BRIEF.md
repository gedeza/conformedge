## 3. What AE SHEQ Gets — ConformEdge Business Tier

### Core Platform
- Up to 25 users (+R199/user beyond)
- All 11 compliance frameworks (ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, 27001, 19011, 31000, 44001, DMRE/MHSA, POPIA, ECSA, SACPCMP)
- Unlimited documents
- 500 AI classifications/month
- API access

### Incident Management (Full Suite)
- Incident reporting with 7 types (near-miss, first aid, medical, lost time, fatality, environmental, property damage)
- **COIDA W.Cl.2 form** auto-generated from incident data
- **SAPS 277 fatality notice** auto-generated
- Evidence/photo uploads with gallery view
- Structured witness statements (name, contact, email, statement)
- MHSA Section 11/23/24 classification with regulatory deadline tracking
- Contributing factors analysis (15 industry-standard factors)
- Lost days tracking for LTIFR calculation
- Body part injured, nature of injury, treatment type fields
- Root cause analysis (Simple + 5-Whys with Ishikawa categories)
- CAPA linking for corrective actions
- Overdue reporting deadline banners

### Safety & Compliance
- Work permits (all 7 types: hot work, confined space, heights, electrical, excavation, lifting, general)
- Compliance checklists with recurring schedules and custom field types
- Subcontractor management with compliance portal
- Gap analysis with AI-powered document classification
- Audit pack generation

### Reporting & Management
- Management review with agenda, actions, attendees
- Objectives & KPI tracking with trend charts
- Reports dashboard with charts and PDF/CSV export
- Audit trail for all actions
- Calendar with scheduled assessments

### Collaboration
