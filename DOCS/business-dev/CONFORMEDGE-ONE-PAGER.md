**Stop losing tenders to disorganised compliance. Start winning them in minutes.**

## The Problem

South African construction and infrastructure companies waste weeks compiling compliance documentation for tenders and audits. Spreadsheets get lost. Certificates expire unnoticed. Audit packs are assembled manually under deadline pressure. Non-compliance findings lead to lost contracts and regulatory penalties.

## The Solution

ConformEdge is an AI-powered platform that centralises your entire compliance operation — documents, assessments, incidents, CAPAs, equipment, and audit packs — in one system built for South African regulations.

---

## Key Features

### AI Document Classification
Upload any document — our AI reads it, identifies the relevant ISO clauses, and files it automatically. No manual tagging. No misfiled records.

### 11 Compliance Frameworks
ISO 9001 | ISO 14001 | ISO 45001 | ISO 22301 | ISO 27001 | ISO 37001 | ISO 39001 | MHSA/DMRE | POPIA | ECSA | SACPCMP

### Incident Management
Full investigation workflow with evidence capture, witness statements, fishbone analysis, COIDA W.Cl.2 export, LTIFR dashboards, and statutory MHSA form generation.

### Audit Packs in One Click
Generate branded, audit-ready PDF packs with cover page, executive summary, compliance evidence, and sign-off — ready for submission in minutes.

### Subcontractor Compliance Portal
Subcontractors upload their own certificates via a secure portal. Expiry alerts notify you 30 days before any cert lapses. Compliance scoring ranks subs by tier.

### Equipment Register
Asset lifecycle management with maintenance schedules, calibration tracking, repair logs, and automatic CAPA escalation from failed inspections.

### Integrated Management System (IMS)
Cross-standard gap analysis, shared clause mapping, and consolidated readiness scoring across all your active standards.

### Mobile-Ready (PWA)
Works on any device — capture data on-site with camera integration, digital signatures, and offline sync.
