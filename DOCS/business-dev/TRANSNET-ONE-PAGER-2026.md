# ConformEdge for Transnet — One-Pager

**AI-Powered Vendor & Contractor Compliance Management**

---

## The Challenge

Transnet manages thousands of vendors, contractors, and service providers across six divisions — Freight Rail, Port Terminals, National Ports Authority, Pipelines, Engineering, and Property. Each project carries different compliance requirements: OHS Act Section 37(2), ISO certifications, BBBEE, COIDA, RSR, and NEMA environmental regulations.

Today, compliance is tracked through spreadsheets, email, and manual document verification. Certificates expire unnoticed. Section 37(2) agreements lapse. Incident data is inconsistent across divisions. The result: regulatory exposure, audit findings, and a rising LTIFR (0.77 in 2024/25).

---

## The Solution

ConformEdge is an AI-powered SHEQ & Compliance Management platform built for South African regulatory requirements. It provides one system to onboard, track, verify, and audit contractor compliance across all divisions and projects.

---

## Key Capabilities

| Area | What ConformEdge Does |
|---|---|
| **OHS Act** | Section 37(2) agreement tracking, SHE file generation, HIRA scheduling, induction records |
| **ISO Standards** | Gap analysis for ISO 45001, 14001, 9001 — cross-standard IMS engine auto-links shared clauses |
| **BBBEE** | Certificate upload, expiry alerts, level tracking, exportable vendor compliance reports |
| **COIDA** | Letter of Good Standing tracking, W.Cl.2 auto-generation, incident investigation with fishbone analysis |
| **Incidents** | Full lifecycle — near-miss to fatality, evidence capture, LTIFR dashboards, trend analysis |
| **Work Permits** | 7 types with digital sign-off, auto-expiry, mobile capture on-site |
| **Equipment** | Asset register, calibration tracking, 13 WRC inspection templates, CAPA escalation |
| **Environmental** | CEMP management, NEMA document tracking, environmental incident reporting |
| **Subcontractor Portal** | Vendors upload their own documents, receive expiry alerts, scored by compliance completeness |
| **Audit Packs** | One-click branded PDF — certification evidence, gap analysis, sign-off — ready in minutes |
| **Mobile** | PWA works offline at ports, rail sites, terminals — camera, signatures, sync |
| **AI** | Upload any document — AI identifies the standard, clause, and files it automatically |
