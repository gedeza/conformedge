## The Problem They Know

Construction in South Africa operates under heavy compliance pressure:

- **CIDB grading** requires demonstrated safety management systems — no system, no tender
- **OHS Act** mandates documented risk assessments, safety files, and incident registers
- **ISO 45001** is the baseline requirement for Tier 1 contractor pre-qualification (Eskom, Transnet, SANRAL, municipalities)
- **Incident reporting** — late W.Cl.2 (COIDA) submissions trigger penalties; fatalities require SAPS 277 within 24 hours
- **Work permits** — hot work, confined space, heights — still managed with carbon-copy books on most sites

**The result:** Safety officers spend more time on admin than on-site safety. Audit prep takes weeks instead of hours.
