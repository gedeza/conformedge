## SLIDE 6 — The Subcontractor Portal

**Let contractors manage their own compliance**

Visual: Portal screenshot or flow diagram

1. Contractor receives a secure portal link
2. They upload: BBBEE cert, COIDA letter, ISO certificates, Section 37(2) agreement, training records
3. System tracks expiry dates — alerts sent 30 days before lapse
4. Compliance score calculated automatically (red/amber/green)
5. Transnet sees all contractor statuses in one dashboard

**Key benefit:** Contractors do the compliance admin. Transnet verifies and monitors.
