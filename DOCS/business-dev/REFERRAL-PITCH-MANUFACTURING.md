## The Problem They Know

SA manufacturers face a compliance triple threat:

- **ISO 9001** (Quality) is the minimum for supplier qualification — most OEMs and retail chains require it
- **ISO 14001** (Environmental) is increasingly mandatory, especially for export markets and ESG reporting
- **ISO 45001** (Safety) — OHS Act compliance plus client requirements for workplace safety systems
- **POPIA** — processing employee data, supplier data, and customer data requires documented information management
- **Audit fatigue** — separate systems for quality, environmental, and safety means preparing for 3 audits independently

**The result:** Quality managers maintain 3+ parallel document systems. Audit prep takes 2-4 weeks. Non-conformances slip through the gaps.

ConformEdge is an **integrated management system (IMS)** that handles multiple ISO standards in one platform:
