## The Problem They Know

Every mining operation in South Africa faces the same compliance pressure:

- **DMRE inspections** are increasing — Section 54 stoppages cost R500K–R5M per day in lost production
- **MHSA Chapter 2** requires documented risk assessments, incident reporting, and safety committees — most mines still track this on spreadsheets and paper logbooks
- **ISO 45001** is increasingly required by mining houses for contractor pre-qualification
- **COIDA reporting** (W.Cl.2 forms) must be submitted within 7 days — late submissions trigger COID Fund penalties
- Fatality rates in SA mining remain among the highest globally — the DMR is tightening enforcement

**The result:** SHEQ managers spend 60%+ of their time on paperwork instead of preventing incidents.
