## 6. Key Platform Capabilities

- **AI-Powered Document Classification & Gap Analysis** — Automated identification of compliance gaps using Claude AI
- **Incident Management** — Full lifecycle with COIDA W.Cl.2 form generation and MHSA statutory reporting
- **Work Permit Management** — 7 permit types covering hot work, confined space, excavation, electrical, working at heights, general, and cold work
- **Equipment Register** — Asset tracking with calibration scheduling, maintenance records, and CAPA escalation
- **Integrated Management System (IMS)** — Multi-standard audits with cross-standard clause mapping
- **Document Approval Workflows** — Configurable review and approval chains
- **Subcontractor Compliance Portal** — External parties submit evidence against assigned requirements
- **Client Portal** — Token-based external sharing for audit evidence and reports
- **Scheduled Assessments & Calendar** — Plan and track internal audits and management reviews
- **Recurring Checklists** — Automated compliance task scheduling
- **Custom Form Builder** — Create bespoke inspection and assessment forms
- **Objectives & KPI Tracking** — Monitor performance against quality and safety targets
- **Management Reviews** — Structured review process with action tracking
- **Mobile PWA** — Offline-capable progressive web application with camera and signature capture
- **PDF Report Generation** — Branded audit reports, incident reports, and management review outputs
- **Role-Based Access Control** — 5 roles: Owner, Admin, Manager, Auditor, Viewer
