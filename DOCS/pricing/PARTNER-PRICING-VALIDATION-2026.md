# Partner Pricing Validation — Research & Analysis

**Date:** 24 March 2026
**Prepared by:** ISU Technologies (Pty) Ltd
**Purpose:** Validate ConformEdge partner pricing against industry benchmarks and SA market reality

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Research revealed that ConformEdge's original flat per-client partner pricing (R899/client with volume discounts up to 20%) was **significantly more generous than industry norms**. At scale (25 clients), the partner captured 82% of value while ConformEdge retained only 18% — compared to an industry standard of 60-80% platform retention.

**Decision:** Replace flat per-client fees + volume discounts with a **tiered per-client pricing model** that keeps entry pricing unchanged but scales ConformEdge's revenue proportionally as partners grow.

## 4. The Problem with the Original Pricing

### Original Model (Flat + Volume Discounts)

Consulting Partner: R4,999/mo base + R899/client flat, with 10% off at 10+, 15% at 25+, 20% at 50+

| Clients | ConformEdge Revenue | Partner Revenue (@ R5K/client) | CE Share | Partner Share |
|---------|--------------------|---------------------------------|----------|---------------|
| 5 | R9,494/mo | R25,000/mo | 38% | 62% |
| 10 | R12,989/mo | R50,000/mo | 26% | 74% |
| 15 | R17,134/mo | R75,000/mo | 23% | 77% |
| 25 | R23,096/mo | R125,000/mo | 18% | 82% |

**Problem:** ConformEdge's share drops from 38% to 18% as the partner grows. The volume discounts compound the issue — rewarding scale with lower per-client fees while ConformEdge provides all the infrastructure, AI, and ongoing development.

**Industry comparison:** At 25 clients, ConformEdge retains 18%. SafetyCulture's best partners retain 70% (platform gets 30%). HubSpot retains 80%. Salesforce retains 65-80%.

---

## 5. The Solution: Tiered Per-Client Pricing

### New Model (Option C — Hybrid Tiered)

Replace flat per-client fees and volume discounts with tiered pricing:

**Consulting Partner:**
- Clients 1–10: R899/mo each (unchanged — no barrier to entry)
- Clients 11–25: R1,299/mo each (partner is proven and profitable)
- Clients 26+: R999/mo each (modest scale reward)

**White-Label Partner:**
- Clients 1–10: R699/mo each (unchanged)
- Clients 11–25: R999/mo each
- Clients 26+: R799/mo each

### Revised Economics (Consulting Partner)

| Clients | ConformEdge Revenue | Partner Revenue (@ R5K/client) | CE Share | Partner Share |
|---------|--------------------|---------------------------------|----------|---------------|
| 5 | R9,494/mo | R25,000/mo | 38% | 62% |
| 10 | R13,989/mo | R50,000/mo | 28% | 72% |
| 15 | R20,484/mo | R75,000/mo | 27% | 73% |
| 25 | R33,474/mo | R125,000/mo | 27% | 73% |
| 30 | R38,469/mo | R150,000/mo | 26% | 74% |

### Improvement Summary

| Clients | Old CE Revenue | New CE Revenue | Increase |
|---------|---------------|---------------|----------|
| 5 | R9,494 | R9,494 | R0 (identical) |
| 10 | R12,989 | R13,989 | +R1,000/mo (+8%) |
| 15 | R17,134 | R20,484 | +R3,350/mo (+20%) |
| 25 | R23,096 | R33,474 | +R10,378/mo (+45%) |

### Why This Works

1. **No barrier to entry** — First 10 clients are identical pricing. Starting partners are unaffected.
2. **ConformEdge scales with the partner** — CE share stabilises at 26-28% instead of dropping to 18%.
3. **Step at client 11 is justified** — By 10 clients, the partner is proven and profitable. R1,299 for client 11+ is easy to absorb when earning R5,000+ per client.
4. **Drop at 26+ rewards true scale** — Partners with 26+ clients get a modest reduction (R1,299 → R999) as loyalty reward, still above the old R899 flat rate.
5. **Simple to explain** — "First 10 at R899, next 15 at R1,299, beyond that R999." No percentage discounts to calculate.

## 7. Talking Points for Partner Conversations

When explaining the pricing to a prospective partner:

### On the Tiered Structure
> "Your first 10 clients are at R899/mo each — the same starting rate. As you grow beyond 10, the per-client rate steps up to R1,299 because you're using more platform capacity and getting more value. At 26+ clients, it drops to R999 as a scale reward. It's simple: the more you grow, the more we both benefit."

### On Value for Money
> "At 15 clients, your total platform cost is about R20,500/mo. If those clients bought ConformEdge individually, they'd pay R82,000/mo combined. You're getting a 75% saving — and you charge them for the full compliance service, not just the software."

### On Why Not Flat Pricing
> "We benchmarked against HubSpot, Salesforce, SafetyCulture, and other SaaS partner programs. A flat rate with deep volume discounts would mean we can't reinvest in the platform as you scale. The tiered model means we grow together — you get more clients, we get the revenue to keep improving the product your clients rely on."

### On the Partner's Total Margins
> "Even with the tiered pricing, your margin per client is R3,700-R6,300/mo after platform costs. With 15 clients, that's R54,500/mo net — before you even add your consulting fees on top. The platform is the most cost-effective part of your entire operation."

### On Practitioner Costs
> "Yes, you'll need an ISO practitioner — typically R20,000-R30,000/mo salary. But split across 15 clients, that's R1,333-R2,000 per client. Combined with the platform cost, your total cost per client is under R3,500/mo — and you're charging R5,000-R8,000. The margins are strong."

## 8. Risk Mitigation

### Why We Changed Before Signing Partners
- Locking in flat R899/client with volume discounts would have been difficult to change later
- Partners build financial models around published pricing — mid-contract changes erode trust
- Better to start with fair pricing than to raise prices on existing partners

### What If a Partner Pushes Back on Tiered Pricing?
- Point to the 5-client economics — identical to before
- Show the annual P&L — R354K/year profit at 15 clients is compelling
- Offer annual prepayment (17% discount) as an alternative incentive
- Remind them: every competitor platform charges 2-3x more for similar partner programs
