## 10. Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Partner margin inversion at scale | Partner earns more than CE at 25+ Pro clients | Flat pricing stabilizes CE share at ~40% — acceptable for channel revenue |
| Referral commission pre-payment | Commission accrued before CE collects full year | Fixed: now accrues monthly per payment received |
| EFT collection delays | Cash flow gap between service and payment | Auto-invoicing 3 days before period end + 7-day grace + auto-suspension |
| Low initial client volume | Revenue doesn't cover fixed costs | Fixed costs are <R31K/mo — break-even at 6 Professional clients |
| Churn before Year 1 for referred clients | Commission paid but client leaves | Commission stops accruing when client churns (monthly accrual model) |
