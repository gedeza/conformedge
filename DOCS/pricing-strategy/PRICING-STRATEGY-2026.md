## Executive Summary

ConformEdge is **significantly underpriced** for the value delivered. Our current R699 Starter tier positions us as a budget tool in a market where buyers equate price with trust and capability. With 31 modules, AI-powered document intelligence, and SA-specific regulatory automation that **no competitor offers**, we should price based on value, not cost.

**Key changes proposed:**
- Raise all tier prices (86-33% increases)
- Add per-user pricing on top of platform fees (hybrid model)
- Create separate Consulting Partner and Government programs
- Increase AI credit pack prices (current pricing leaves margin on the table)
- Update landing page standards count (7 → 9) and sub-clause count (187 → 227+)

## 2. Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based, Not Cost-Based

Our AI document classification alone saves 15-30 minutes per document. A company processing 200 documents/month saves 50-100 hours of compliance officer time. At R200-R350/hour loaded cost, that's **R10,000-R35,000/mo in labor savings from a single feature**.

A single workplace incident poorly documented can result in DoEL fines starting at R50,000. Our incident management + statutory form generation provides insurance against this.

### Hybrid Model: Platform Fee + Per-User Seats + AI Credits

| Component | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Platform fee** | Captures baseline value of compliance system |
| **Per-user seats** | Scales revenue with organization size |
| **AI credits** | Aligns cost with value; creates expansion revenue |

### 14.2 AI Credit Economy (Usage-Based Expansion)

#### What Happens Per Credit

Each AI credit represents one document classification:
1. User uploads a document (PDF, Word, Excel, or scanned image)
2. Claude Haiku AI reads the content (~2,000–4,000 tokens)
3. AI maps it to the correct ISO standard and clause with confidence scoring
4. Returns: standard match, clause mapping, confidence score, gap insights

#### Unit Economics Per Classification

| Component | Tokens | Cost (USD) | Cost (ZAR ~R18.50) |
|-----------|--------|-----------|---------------------|
| Input (document text) | ~3,000 | $0.00075 | R0.014 |
| Output (classification result) | ~500 | $0.000625 | R0.012 |
| **Total API cost per call** | | **~$0.0014** | **~R0.026** |

#### Credit Pack Pricing vs Cost

| Pack | Price | Per Credit | Our Cost | Margin | Markup |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|--------|--------|
| 100 credits | R25 | R0.25 | R0.026 | R0.224 | 9.6x |
| 500 credits | R99 | R0.198 | R0.026 | R0.172 | 7.6x |
| 1,000 credits | R179 | R0.179 | R0.026 | R0.153 | 6.9x |
| 5,000 credits | R749 | R0.15 | R0.026 | R0.124 | 5.8x |

#### Value to Customer (ROI Justification)

Even at R0.25/credit, the customer pays R0.25 to save R50–R175 of SHEQ officer time:

| Manual Classification | AI Classification |
|----------------------|-------------------|
| SHEQ officer reads document (10 min) | Upload document (30 seconds) |
| Cross-references against ISO standard (10 min) | AI classifies instantly |
| Maps to correct clause (5 min) | Returns clause + confidence score |
| **Total: 15–30 minutes** | **Total: ~30 seconds** |
| **Cost: R50–R175** (at R200–R350/hr) | **Cost: R0.25** |

**ROI per classification: 200–700x return on investment for the customer.**
