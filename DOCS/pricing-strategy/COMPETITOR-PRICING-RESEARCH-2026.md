## 4. Key Findings

### Finding 1: ConformEdge is Extremely Underpriced for Feature Set
ConformEdge's Professional tier at $162/mo for 10 users delivers more functionality (31 modules, AI classification, IMS engine, SA regulatory, work permits, subcontractor portal) than platforms charging $800–$2,500/mo for comparable user counts. The effective per-user cost of $13–$16 is below even SafetyCulture's inspection-only tool.

### Finding 2: No Competitor Offers AI + SA Regulatory + Multi-Standard IMS
- **AI document classification:** Only SafetyCulture offers any AI (template builder). No competitor has AI document classification or gap analysis.
- **SA regulatory (DMRE/MHSA, POPIA):** Only IsoMetrix and Ariscu touch SA regulations, both at significantly higher price points.
- **Union-Find IMS engine:** Unique to ConformEdge. No competitor offers automated cross-standard equivalence mapping.

### Finding 3: Market Tolerates Significantly Higher Prices
- Growth-stage companies (15–25 users) routinely pay $2,500/mo ($30K/yr) for basic QMS
- Enterprise compliance budgets run $50K–$150K/yr for GRC platforms
- SA enterprise buyers (IsoMetrix clients) pay R18,500/mo+ for less functionality
- ConformEdge Business at R5,999/mo ($324/mo) is still 87% cheaper than the market average for comparable feature sets

### Finding 4: Per-User Pricing is the Dominant Model
Most competitors (SafetyCulture, Isolocity, Greenlight Guru, MasterControl, SHEQX) use per-user pricing. ConformEdge's flat-tier model with included users is more predictable for buyers but may leave revenue on the table for larger deployments.

### Finding 5: Implementation Fees are Standard
- MasterControl: $10,000–$100,000+ implementation
- Intelex: $5,000–$50,000+ implementation
- Qualio: $8,000–$20,000 setup fee
- ConformEdge currently charges no implementation fee, which is a significant competitive advantage for SMBs but may undervalue the onboarding effort.

### Finding 6: Annual Discounts are Universal
Most platforms offer 10–20% annual payment discounts. ConformEdge's 17% (pay 10 for 12) is in line with industry standard.

## 5. Pricing Recommendations

### Short-Term (Pre-Presentation, 13 March 2026)
Current pricing is defensible for market entry and early adoption. The low price point reduces friction for SA construction/infrastructure companies evaluating the platform.

### Medium-Term (Post-Launch, Q2 2026)
Consider:
1. **Raise Professional to R3,999/mo** — still 85% below comparable platforms
2. **Raise Business to R7,999/mo** — still 80% below IsoMetrix
3. **Add per-user overage pricing** — R199/user/mo beyond tier limit
4. **Add implementation/onboarding fee** — R4,999 one-time (optional, waived for annual)
5. **Introduce SA Regulatory Add-on** — R999/mo for DMRE/MHSA + POPIA modules (included in Business+)

### Long-Term (2027+)
- Enterprise pricing should target R15,000–R25,000/mo to compete with IsoMetrix
- Consulting Partner program at R3,999/mo + R799/client is well-positioned
- Government annual licensing at R120K–R500K/yr aligns with public sector procurement budgets
